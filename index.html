A Conversation with Your Data

Use Case: Marketing Analytics

The Last-Minute Marketing Report

Situation: Imena, a Marketing Lead, needs to justify ad spend for a report due in an hour. The data analyst is unavailable.

Action: She drags her campaign CSV into QueryBoard and asks: "What was the cost per lead for the 'Tap & Go' campaign vs the 'Mobile Money' campaign?"

Outcome: An instant chart shows the 'Tap & Go' campaign was 30% more efficient. She adds the visual to her report and submits it with confidence.

Use Case: Sales Performance

The Proactive Sales Coach

Situation: David, a Sales Manager, is preparing for team one-on-ones. He wants to identify specific coaching opportunities, not just overall performance.

Action: He connects to the live sales database and asks: "Show the month-over-month sales growth for each rep in the Western Province."

Monetization Strategy

Our strategy is built to serve our diverse user base, from individuals to corporate entities, by aligning price with value through product-led growth.

  • Freemium Core for Individuals: A generous free tier empowers individuals and encourages widespread adoption and feedback.
  • Future Premium Tiers for Teams: Paid plans will offer the collaborative features, direct database connections, and enhanced security that growing businesses require.
  • Enterprise Solutions: Custom plans for large organizations will focus on scalability, advanced security integrations, and dedicated support.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: React & TypeScript for a dynamic and reliable user interface.
  • Backend & Preprocessing: Python (FastAPI) for high-performance APIs. We use libraries like Pandas for robust in-memory data manipulation.
  • AI/ML Core: Fine-tuned LLMs that translate natural language into executable Pandas queries for file-based data analysis.
  • Contextual Understanding: Upon upload, a schema graph generation process maps data columns and relationships, providing crucial context to the AI for more accurate and intelligent query generation.
  • Infrastructure: Deployed on AWS for scalability, reliability, and global reach.
